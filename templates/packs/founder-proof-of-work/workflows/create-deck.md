# Workflow: Create Deck

Inputs:

- `index.md`
- `templates/weekly-update.md`
- reviewed artifacts under `outputs/`

Deck shape:

1. Problem
2. Customer
3. Product
4. Evidence of progress
5. Metrics
6. Why now
7. Ask or next step

Rules:

- Prefer specific evidence over broad claims.
- Keep private details out of public decks.
- Preserve source references in speaker notes or appendix.
